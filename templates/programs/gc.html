Our Council

The University Council of the University of Gold Coast comprise a distinguished assembly of eminent global leaders, representing diverse continents and an array of professional domains, including commerce, industry, academia, and governance. This esteemed body brings together a wealth of experience, visionary leadership, and a broad spectrum of expertise, providing strategic guidance and fostering the university’s mission of excellence. Their collective insight and global perspective serve as a cornerstone of UGC’s competitive advantage, enabling the institution to navigate the complexities of a rapidly evolving educational landscape. Drawing on their backgrounds in commerce and industry, these leaders offer invaluable input on aligning academic programs with market demands, ensuring that UGC remains at the forefront of innovation and relevance. Furthermore, their international reach and professional networks enhance the university’s ability to forge strategic partnerships, attract world-class faculty, and cultivate opportunities for global collaboration. By integrating academic rigor with industry-aligned strategies, the Council and Board of Trustees play a pivotal role in shaping UGC as a premier institution that bridges the gap between academia and the professional world. This dynamic leadership structure not only reinforces the university’s governance framework but also propels its vision of producing servant leaders equipped to address global challenges with integrity, expertise, and purpose. Through their dedication and strategic oversight, the University of Gold Coast continues to set a benchmark for transformative education in Africa and beyond.
